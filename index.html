Good to know
Why Dratflix?
We are a dedicated team of experts. That's your biggest advantage: We are the most motivated partners you'll find. We work around the clock on your project and won't rest until you're 100% happy. Direct contact, no waiting, maximum dedication.
How long does it take?
My motto: "Faster live, faster growth". Simple projects are often completed in 1-2 weeks. Complex platforms require more time depending on scope.
How much does a website cost?
All prices are calculated individually on request. Every project is unique and priced based on your specific requirements and scope. Contact me for a free, no-obligation quote.
What technologies does Dratflix use?
We primarily develop with Next.js 16, React 19, TypeScript, and Tailwind CSS. Backend: Supabase, Stripe for payments. All websites are optimized for PageSpeed 100/100.
Are Dratflix websites SEO-optimized?
Yes, basic SEO is always included: Structured data (Schema.org), meta tags, sitemap, performance optimization, and mobile responsive design.
Do you offer in-person consultations?
Yes, personal meetings can be arranged if necessary. However, we primarily work digitally via video calls — flexible to your schedule.
Why not use a page builder like Wix or Squarespace?
Page builders are simple but limited. My custom development offers: Unlimited customization, better performance (PageSpeed 100 vs. 50-70), professional SEO, and unique design.
